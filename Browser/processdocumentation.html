Process Documentation

Overview

Since we are working with transaction data, we created a Postgres Database in Heroku. This allowed us to share the database without needing to recreate a Postgres database on individual computers

Our visualization is Flask-powered and we've used a combination of Leaflet, Plotly, Font Awesome, Folium and DimpleJS (new JavaScript libraries)
